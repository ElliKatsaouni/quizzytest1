Welcome to Quizzy!

Here are some FAQs about this site.

What is Quizzy?

Pub quiz lovers and soon to be pub quiz lovers finally have a place to find new quizzes and pubs in Glasgow, Edinburgh or Aberdeen. Join us on building a network from quiz lovers for quiz lovers. Add quizzes you love. Rate quizzes you have been to. Explore new quizzes in your city every week. We will provide you with the details, so just come and visit us for many quizzy nights to come!

How do I use Quizzy?

Just explore pubs and their quizzes in our available cities or use the search bar if you are searching for a specific pub. Click on the pub and see when quizzes take place there or use the weekday filter to search for quizzes on particular days.

Can't find your pub?

If your favorite pub and pub quiz are not yet registered on this site, use the "Add pub" function to add the pub and its details and then the "Add Quiz" function to add some quiz details by yourself. It's easy and just takes 5 minutes!

Why can I not add pubs and quizzes and why can I not rate them?

In order to rate quizzes or add new content and comments, you need to be a registered user. Just create an account - it's easy! And did you know? If you sign up, you can save your favorite quizzes in your account, leave comments and rate the quizzes you have already been to.

Who did this awesome page?!

Quizzy is brought to you by The Quizmasters. We are four postgraduate students of Glasgow University. We love pub quizzes as much as you do and had to google our way through lots and lots of – partly awful looking – pages or the pub’s Facebook streams to find the right pub quiz at the right time. Annoying! So we tried to provide a solution for this.

A special thanks goes to Gerardo and David for teaching us how to do all of this...
